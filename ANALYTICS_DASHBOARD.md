## üìà ANALYTICS INTERPRETATION GUIDE

### Good vs Bad Metrics:

**Instagram**:
- ‚úÖ Good engagement: 5-8%
- ‚ùå Low engagement: <1%
- ‚úÖ Good reach: 20-40% of followers
- ‚úÖ Good saves: More than likes means valuable content

**TikTok**:
- ‚úÖ Good completion rate: 50%+
- ‚ùå Poor completion: <30%
- ‚úÖ Good engagement: 3% (likes+shares/views)
- ‚úÖ Growing followers: 10%+ weekly

**Pinterest**:
- ‚úÖ Good outbound clicks: 100+ per pin
- ‚úÖ Good CTR: 2-5%
- ‚ùå Poor CTR: <1%
- ‚úÖ Monthly viewers growing: 20%+ monthly

**Email**:
- ‚úÖ Good open rate: 20-30%
- ‚úÖ Good click rate: 2-5%
- ‚ùå Poor engagement: <15% open rate
- ‚úÖ Low unsubscribe: <0.5%

**Website**:
- ‚úÖ Conversion rate: 1-3%
- ‚úÖ Social traffic: 30%+ of total
- ‚úÖ Low bounce rate: <50%
- ‚úÖ Session duration: 2+ minutes

## üìâ RED FLAGS TO WATCH

**Stop Doing This**:
- ‚ùå Post getting <1% engagement consistently
- ‚ùå Content with >60% bounce rate
- ‚ùå Videos with <30% completion rate
- ‚ùå Email campaigns with <10% open rate
- ‚ùå Traffic sources with 0% conversion

**When to Pivot Strategy**:
- Instagram Reels underperforming ‚Üí Focus on Carousels
- TikTok videos not viral ‚Üí Analyze trending sounds/hashtags
- Pinterest traffic but no sales ‚Üí Improve product links
- Email open rate dropping ‚Üí Change subject lines
- Overall traffic flat ‚Üí Increase content frequency or quality

## üí° ANALYTICS INSIGHTS QUESTIONS

Ask yourself weekly:

1. **What's working?**
   - Which content type gets most engagement?
   - Which platform drives most sales?
   - Which message resonates most?

2. **What's not working?**
   - Which posts consistently underperform?
   - Which platform costs most vs return?
   - Why are people not clicking?

3. **What should we do more of?**
   - Reels? Stories? Carousels?
   - Product focus? Educational? Behind-the-scenes?
   - Daily? Weekly? Different schedule?

4. **What should we stop doing?**
   - Unpopular content format
   - Posts at wrong times
   - Low-ROI activities

5. **What can we test?**
   - Different hashtags
   - Different posting times
   - Different captions/CTAs
   - Different visuals
