## üéØ NEGOTIATION TIPS

### What to Ask For:

1. **Content Deliverables**
   - How many posts?
   - Which formats? (Feed, Story, Reel)
   - Timeline?
   - Hashtags/mentions?

2. **Rights & Usage**
   - Can you repost on TreeOfLifa channels?
   - How long stays published?
   - Can you use in ads/marketing?

3. **Tracking**
   - Unique discount code? (to track sales)
   - UTM link? (to track clicks)
   - Affiliate link setup?

4. **Expectations**
   - Authenticity: Real opinion (not robotic)
   - Transparency: Disclose ad partnership
   - Timing: When will content go live?

### What NOT to Ask For:

‚ùå Exclusive content (unless paying premium)
‚ùå Long-term commitment immediately
‚ùå Content ownership (they own their content)
‚ùå Guaranteed engagement numbers
‚ùå Fake reviews/exaggerated claims

### Negotiation Strategy:

**Starting Point**: 
- Micro: Free products + 15% commission
- Macro: Free products + 10% commission OR ‚Ç¨300-500 flat

**If They Negotiate**:
- Increase commission: 20% (instead of paying flat fee)
- OR increase flat fee, reduce timeline
- OR extend partnership (3-6 months vs 1 month)

**Always**: Get everything in writing (DM, email, or simple contract)

## üöÄ INFLUENCER OUTREACH CAMPAIGN TIMELINE

### Month 1: Building List & Reaching Out

**Week 1**: Identify 50+ micro-influencers
- Use HypeAuditor, search hashtags
- Document in spreadsheet
- Check follower authenticity (no bot accounts)

**Week 2**: Send first wave of outreach (15-20 emails)
- Personalized, not generic
- Include product images
- Clear call-to-action

**Week 3**: Follow-ups to non-responders
- Send 5-10 follow-ups
- Try different angles
- Maybe send DMs on Instagram

**Week 4**: Compile responses & negotiate
- Track who's interested
- Negotiate terms
- Send first batch of products

### Month 2: Content Creation & Tracking

**Week 1-2**: 
- Monitor product arrivals
- Respond to influencer questions
- Prepare unique discount codes

**Week 2-4**:
- Content starts rolling in
- Track engagement & sales
- Repost best content
- Thank influencers publicly

### Month 3: Analysis & Round 2

- Analyze ROI from month 1
- Which influencers performed best?
- Adjust strategy (more budget to top performers)
- Reach out to 20 more influencers
- Plan long-term partnerships with best collaborators
