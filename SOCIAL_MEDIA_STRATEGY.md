## üéØ Platform Priority & Strategy

### 1. **Instagram** (PRIORITY #1 - Best ROI for natural/eco products)
**Why**: Visual platform, eco-conscious audience, high engagement for beauty/wellness

#### Content Pillars:
1. **Product Showcase** (40% of posts)
   - Close-ups of loofah texture, natural details
   - Before/after skin results
   - Product lifestyle shots (in bathroom, hands using product)
   - Post ideas:
     - "Feel the difference: Real loofah texture" (carousel showing growth process)
     - "These hands created your skincare routine" (behind-the-scenes)
     - Customer testimonials with results

2. **Educational Content** (30% of posts)
   - Benefits of natural exfoliation
   - How to use each product properly
   - Sustainable living tips
   - Post ideas:
     - "Why loofahs beat plastic: 5 benefits"
     - "How often should you exfoliate?" (carousel)
     - "The journey from seed to spa" (loofah growth)
     - Skincare myths vs facts

3. **Sustainability & Behind-the-Scenes** (20% of posts)
   - Local supplier stories
   - Eco-friendly packaging
   - Sourcing practices
   - Post ideas:
     - "Meet our farmers" (with photos)
     - "Zero-waste packaging explained"
     - Your personal story - why you started TreeOfLifa

4. **User-Generated Content & Community** (10% of posts)
   - Customer reviews & photos
   - Customer testimonials
   - Sustainability challenges

#### Instagram Content Calendar (Weekly):
- **Monday**: Educational tip (Carousel)
- **Wednesday**: Product showcase with lifestyle photo
- **Friday**: Behind-the-scenes or sustainability story
- **Sunday**: UGC or customer testimonial

#### Hashtag Strategy:
**Branded**: #TreeOfLifa #TreeOfLifaSE #NaturalSkincare
**Product**: #LuffaSvamp #EcologicalBeauty #NaturalExfoliation #AleppoSoap
**Lifestyle**: #SustainableLiving #EcoBeauty #NaturalSkinCare #CleanBeauty
**Local**: #SvenskNaturv√•rd #MadeinSweden #LocalSustainable

**Use 25-30 hashtags per post (mix of high/low volume)**

#### Growth Tactics:
- Post consistently (3x/week minimum)
- Engage with similar accounts daily (10-15 mins)
- Use Reels for reach (Instagram algorithm favors video)
- Cross-promote with eco/wellness accounts
- Run monthly giveaway campaigns
- Collaborate with micro-influencers (10k-50k followers)

### 2. **TikTok** (PRIORITY #2 - Fast viral growth potential)
**Why**: Younger audience, algorithmic reach, perfect for DIY/eco content

#### Content Ideas (15-60 seconds):
1. **"POV" Videos**
   - "POV: You're trying to switch to sustainable skincare"
   - "POV: Your loofah's last day before composting"

2. **Trends with TreeOfLifa Spin**
   - Use trending sounds with product transformation clips
   - "It's giving natural skincare" trend

3. **Educational Quick Tips**
   - "3 ways to use a loofah"
   - "Why your plastic loofah is damaging your skin"

4. **ASMR Content** (high engagement)
   - Loofah texture sounds
   - Soap sudsing up
   - Satisfying product shots

5. **Before/After transformations**
   - Skin texture improvements
   - Customer testimonials

#### TikTok Strategy:
- Post 4-5x/week
- Use trending sounds & hashtags
- Post at 6-9 PM (peak times)
- Respond to all comments within 1 hour
- Create a TikTok series (e.g., "#NaturalSkinJourney")

#### Hashtags for TikTok:
#NaturalSkincare #EcoFriendly #SustainableLiving #BeautyHacks #CleanBeauty #FYP #Foryoupage

### 3. **Pinterest** (PRIORITY #3 - High-intent traffic)
**Why**: Users searching for solutions, drives website traffic, long content lifespan

#### Content Strategy:
1. **Product Pins** (high CTR)
   - "10 Natural Exfoliation Methods"
   - "Best Eco-Friendly Skincare Products"
   - "Sustainable Gift Ideas for Her"

2. **Blog/Article Pins** (create these articles on your site first)
   - "Complete Guide to Natural Loofah Benefits"
   - "Eco-Friendly Skincare Routine for Sensitive Skin"
   - "How to Create Zero-Waste Beauty Routine"

3. **Infographic Pins**
   - Benefits comparison (loofah vs plastic)
   - Skincare step-by-step guides
   - Sustainability statistics

#### Pinterest Best Practices:
- Create 50+ pins (multiple designs per article)
- Pin regularly: 5-15 pins/day (use scheduling tool)
- Use rich pins (product pins with price/availability)
- Link pins to product pages & blog articles
- Join group boards in eco/beauty niche

---

### 4. **YouTube** (PRIORITY #4 - Long-term authority & SEO)
**Why**: SEO value, builds authority, video content performs well

#### Video Content Ideas:
1. **Product Reviews & Demos** (2-3 min)
   - "Aleppo Soap Review: What 100 Customers Say"
   - "Loofah Lifespan: How Long Does It Really Last?"

2. **Tutorials** (5-7 min)
   - "How to Use a Loofah Properly"
   - "DIY Natural Exfoliation Routine"
   - "Sustainable Skincare Setup Tour"

3. **Behind-the-Scenes** (3-5 min)
   - "From Farm to Your Door: Loofah Journey"
   - "Sustainable Packaging Process"

4. **Customer Testimonials** (2-3 min)
   - Real customers sharing results
   - Before/after skin transformations

#### YouTube Strategy:
- Upload 1-2 videos/week initially
- Optimize titles, descriptions, tags for SEO
- Create playlists (Reviews, Tutorials, Behind-the-Scenes)
- Add links to your website in video descriptions
- Create YouTube shorts (short-form content)

---

### 5. **Facebook** (PRIORITY #5 - Niche community building)
**Why**: Older demographic, group building, community engagement

#### Content Strategy:
- Share blog articles
- Run targeted ads
- Create engagement posts (polls, questions)
- Host Facebook Live Q&A sessions
- Build a community around sustainability

#### Facebook Ads Best Practices:
- Target: Women 25-55, eco-conscious, interested in skincare
- Lookalike audiences based on website visitors
- Test different ad creatives (carousel ads with products)
- Budget: Start with $5-10/day to test
