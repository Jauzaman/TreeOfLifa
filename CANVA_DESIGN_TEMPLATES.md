## üí° DESIGN TIPS & TRICKS

### Typography Rules
- Use max 2-3 fonts per design
- Sans-serif for modern look (Montserrat, Poppins, Inter)
- Serif for classic/traditional (Georgia, Playfair)
- Minimum 24pt for body text (mobile readable)
- 48pt+ for headlines

### Color Psychology
- Green: Trust, nature, growth, health
- White: Clean, minimal, premium
- Gold: Luxury, quality, premium
- Black/Dark Green: Strong, authoritative

### Layout Principles
- Rule of thirds (divide into 3x3 grid)
- White space (15-20% of design)
- Visual hierarchy (largest = most important)
- Alignment (left, center, or right - be consistent)
- Contrast (text vs background)

### Mobile-Friendly Design
- Safe zone: Leave 50px margin on all sides
- Test on phone before publishing
- Text readable at 6"+ distance
- Tap targets min 44x44px
- Load times under 3 seconds
