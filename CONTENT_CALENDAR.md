## üì≤ SCHEDULING PLATFORMS

### Recommended (Free/Cheap):

**Meta Business Suite** (FREE for Facebook + Instagram)
- Schedule up to 90 days in advance
- Best for: Instagram feed posts
- Timezone: Automatic
- Download: Meta.com

**Buffer** ($5-15/month)
- Support: IG, TikTok, Pinterest, Twitter, LinkedIn
- Best for: Multi-platform scheduling
- Analytics: Basic included
- Website: buffer.com

**Later** ($15-50/month)
- Instagram, Pinterest, TikTok, LinkedIn
- Visual calendar view
- Best for: Visual planning
- Website: later.com

**Hootsuite** ($49+/month)
- All platforms supported
- Team collaboration
- Advanced analytics
- Website: hootsuite.com

### TikTok Scheduling
- TikTok Creator Fund members can schedule
- Upload to gallery, schedule through app
- Or use Buffer (limited support)
- Best practice: Post live at 6-9 PM

### Pinterest Scheduling
- Native scheduling in Pinterest app
- Schedule 100+ pins per day
- Best for: Content library building
- Use "Create a pin" ‚Üí Schedule
