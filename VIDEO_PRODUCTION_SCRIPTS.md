## üé® TECHNICAL FILMING TIPS

### Camera Settings

**iPhone:**
- Video format: ProRes (if available)
- Resolution: 4K (1080p minimum)
- Frame rate: 24fps or 30fps
- Lighting: Natural window light 3-5 hours before sunset
- Focus: Tap to lock on product
- White balance: Auto or "cool" for natural look

**Android:**
- Settings ‚Üí Camera ‚Üí Resolution: 4K if available
- Video stabilization: ON
- Focus: Continuous
- Audio: Automatic
- Light: Same as iPhone advice

### Lighting Setup (Budget-Friendly)

**Setup 1 - Window Light** (FREE)
- Position product near window
- Diffuse harsh light with white sheet
- Reflector: Use white poster board opposite window
- Best time: 3-5pm (golden hour)

**Setup 2 - Ring Light** (‚Ç¨50)
- Mount above camera pointing down at 45¬∞
- Illuminates product evenly
- Reduces shadows
- Good for consistent indoor shoots

**Setup 3 - Three-Point Lighting** (‚Ç¨100+)
- Key light: Main light source (side, 45¬∞ angle)
- Fill light: Bounce light or reflector opposite
- Back light: Light behind product for depth
- Looks professional, YouTube-quality

### Audio Recording

**Best Approach:**
- Record separate audio if possible
- Use phone mic 6-12 inches from speaker
- Minimize background noise
- Record in quiet room (avoid echoes)
- Use trending TikTok audio in editing

**CapCut Audio:**
- Add trending sounds from TikTok library
- Adjust volume in timeline
- Sync with visual beats
- Add voiceover after filming

## ‚úÇÔ∏è EDITING IN CAPCUT (FREE)

### Basic Workflow

1. **Import Footage**
   - Open CapCut
   - Create new project (9:16 for TikTok/Reels)
   - Import raw video clips

2. **Cut & Arrange**
   - Trim long shots to 2-4 seconds each
   - Remove mistakes/pauses
   - Arrange in story order
   - Preview timeline

3. **Add Music**
   - Add audio ‚Üí Trending sounds
   - Search by platform (TikTok, Instagram, etc.)
   - Place music track on timeline
   - Adjust volume (voiceover underneath?)

4. **Add Text**
   - Click "Text" button
   - Add opening hook (0-3 seconds)
   - Add key points (bold, readable font)
   - Add CTA at end
   - Font: Modern sans-serif (Montserrat, Poppins)
   - Color: White with black outline = readable everywhere

5. **Add Transitions**
   - Between major sections
   - Fade/cut most common
   - Don't overuse (distract from product)
   - 0.3-0.5 second transitions

6. **Effects** (Optional)
   - Slow-motion: Good for satisfying shots
   - Zoom: Highlights important details
   - Filters: Minimal - keep product looking real

7. **Export**
   - Format: MP4
   - Resolution: 1080p minimum (4K if possible)
   - Frame rate: 30fps
   - Keep original aspect ratio

### Common CapCut Mistakes to Avoid

‚ùå Too much text on screen
‚ùå Text too small to read
‚ùå Shaky footage
‚ùå Poor lighting
‚ùå Music doesn't match mood
‚ùå Too many transitions

## üìä ANALYTICS & OPTIMIZATION

**Track These Metrics:**
- Watch time (how long people watch)
- Completion rate (% who watch until end)
- Engagement (likes, shares, comments)
- Click-through rate (people visiting store)
- Average view duration per platform

**Optimize Based On:**
- If watch time <50%: Hook isn't working, edit opening
- If completion <30%: Too long or boring, cut/speed up
- If no clicks: CTA unclear or no CTA visible
- If low engagement: Product isn't compelling, change angle

**A/B Testing Ideas:**
- Try 3 versions of thumbnail
- Test 2 different music tracks
- Film with different backgrounds
- Try different text overlay styles
- Test opening hooks
